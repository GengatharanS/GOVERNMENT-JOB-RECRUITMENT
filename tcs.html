INFORMATION ABOUT TNPSC

TNPSC Group 1 is a recruitment exam conducted by the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission (TNPSC). The TNPSC conducts various recruitment exams for Group 1,2,3,4 vacancies in the Tamil Nadu state administration. As per the current update in the TNPSC exam, it has been made mandatory for the candidates to furnish their Aadhaar number at the time of applying for the Commission’s exams. Fingerprints of the candidates will be verified with the biometric data available with Aadhaar and only then will they be allowed to sit for exams in future.

A list of TNPSC Exams in 2020 is given below followed by that of 2019: 1.Combined Civil Services Examination – I (Group 1 Services) 2.Combined Engineering Subordinate Services Examination 3.Combined Library and Information Services Examination 4.Combined Geology Service Examination 5.Combined Statistics Service Examination 6.Combined Civil Services Examination – II (Group-II And Group-II A Services 7.Combined Civil Services Examination-III (Group-III Services) 8.Combined Civil Services Examination-IV (Group-IV Services & VAO) The commission has recently released TNPSC Group 1 result which candidates can check in the linked article. A list of TNPSC Exams in 2019 is given below: 1.Combined Civil Services Examination – I (Group 1 Services) 2.Combined Engineering Subordinate Services Examination 3.Combined Engineering Services Examination 4.Combined Civil Services Examination – II (Group 2 and 2 A Services) 5.Combined Civil Services Examination – IV (Group 4 Services and VAO) For the complete TNPSC Exam Calendar 2020, candidates should check the official website (www.tnpsc.gov.in) of the commission or check the TNPSC Group 1 Notification in the linked article.
